# REVENGE - Anime Movie Project

## 🎬 Project Overview

**REVENGE** is an epic anime movie project featuring an intense narrative of betrayal, action, and ultimate vengeance. The story follows two powerful emperors and their loyal followers as they navigate a world filled with danger, deception, and deadly confrontations.

## 📖 Story Summary

A group of 13 friends established two powerful emperors - **Genjutsu** and **Nightmare** - to help people in need. However, law enforcement viewed them as a threat and launched a plan to capture them. Among the group, a black sheep betrayed their location to the police, setting off a chain of events that led to arrests, daring rescues, and a tragic ending.

### Key Plot Points:
- Formation of two empires by 13 friends
- Police surveillance and capture attempts
- Betrayal from within the group
- Daring rescue missions
- Intense action sequences (car chases, sniper battles, building infiltrations)
- Final confrontation with the betrayer
- Tragic conclusion

## 👥 Main Characters

### Genjutsu Emperor
- **Role:** Leader of the Genjutsu faction
- **Skills:** Master of illusions and strategic warfare
- **Members:** 6 skilled operatives with diverse abilities

### Nightmare Emperor
- **Role:** Leader of the Nightmare faction
- **Skills:** Fear-based tactics and psychological manipulation
- **Members:** 6 skilled operatives with specialized combat skills
